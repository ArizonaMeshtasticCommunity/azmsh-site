# Reach Out

We would love to hear from you! Whether you are brand new to Meshtastic or a seasoned operator, the community is here to help.

---

### Join the Discord

Our Discord server is the most active place to connect with the Arizona Meshtastic community. This is where you will find setup help, the Arizona-specific preset and frequency settings, and discussion about node placement and network health.
